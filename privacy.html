Privacy Policy

Your data, plainly explained.

Effective: 10 May 2026

The short version

If you give us your email through the waitlist form, we'll use it to tell you when Hailsi launches. We don't sell it, share it with advertisers, or use tracking cookies. You can unsubscribe or ask us to delete you anytime.

Who we are

Hailsi is in private development by an independent developer. You can reach us at hello@hailsi.com.

What we collect

Just your email address — and only when you submit it through the waitlist form on this site.

We don't use tracking cookies. We don't run third-party analytics like Google Analytics. We don't fingerprint visitors. The only thing the marketing site stores about you is whatever your browser cached on your own machine.

What we do with your email

We use it to:

  • Send you a single notification when Hailsi v1 ships.
  • Optionally, send you occasional development updates — but only if you've explicitly opted in to those.

That's it. We don't sell, rent, or trade your email. We don't share it with advertisers or partners. We don't use it for anything you didn't sign up for.

Where it's stored

Email addresses are stored at MailerLite, our email service provider. MailerLite has its own privacy policy, is GDPR-compliant, and stores data on servers in the European Union. MailerLite is contractually bound to handle your data only for the purposes we've authorized.

Your rights

Wherever you live, you can:

  • Unsubscribe at any time — every email we send has an unsubscribe link.
  • Email hello@hailsi.com to request deletion of your data.
  • Email us to request a copy of what we have on file (which is, at most, your email address and the date you signed up).

If you're in the EU or UK, the GDPR gives you these rights formally. If you're in California, the CCPA gives you similar rights. We honor them regardless of where you live.

Children's privacy

Hailsi is not directed to children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has submitted their email through our form, please email us and we'll delete the record.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your email from loss or unauthorized access. The site itself runs on Cloudflare with HTTPS enforced. MailerLite handles encryption at rest. No system is bulletproof, but the surface area here is small — we only have your email.

Updates to this policy

If we change this policy in any meaningful way, we'll email subscribers and update the date at the top of this page. Trivial wording fixes won't trigger a notification.

Contact

Questions? Email hello@hailsi.com.
